Crop is Healthy

Corn crop is in early vegetative stage (V3–V5) and showing healthy leaf development with no visible disease or major pest damage detected at this time.

🌿
Follow Split Fertilizer Application

Do not apply all fertilizer at once. Use the 3-split method for Urea and 2-split for MOP. This improves nutrient use efficiency and reduces leaching losses.

🚨
Watch Closely for Fall Armyworm

Fall Armyworm is the most dangerous pest for corn. Scout twice a week. Install pheromone traps now. If spotted, immediately apply Chlorantraniliprole 18.5 SC @ 150 ml/ha.

💧
Maintain Regular Irrigation

Do not let the soil dry out. During tasseling and silking (Week 8–10), even a single day of water stress can reduce yield by up to 10%. Irrigate every 2–3 days then.

🪱
Improve Soil with Organic Matter

Organic matter is medium. Add 5–10 tons/ha of well-decomposed FYM or vermicompost before the next crop cycle. This improves water retention, aeration, and microbial activity.

🌤️
Climate is Supporting Good Yield

Current temperature and humidity are within the acceptable range for corn growth. Maintain irrigation to buffer any heat stress. Expected yield: 5–8 tons/hectare with proper management.
